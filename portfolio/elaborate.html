Description

Elaborate is a business that provides a platform for doctors to send modern lab results to patients in an easy-to-understand format, including information on what the results mean and what actions should be taken next. This results in less administrative time for doctors and fewer panicked phone calls for patients, leading to a smoother and more efficient healthcare experience for all.

Problem / Task

The client for this project had a highly detailed-oriented focus and placed a significant emphasis on achieving perfect pixel development that aligned with their design. Additionally, they required the website to function seamlessly across all devices, with an emphasis on speed optimization. I was able to meet these demands by utilizing my expertise in responsive web design, web development and optimization techniques, resulting in a website that met and exceeded the client's expectations.
